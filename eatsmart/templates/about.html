{% blocktrans %}How often have you read a great Yelp review for a restaurant only to walk in and see that it's got a dismal public health inspection score? If you're the kind of person who pays attention to those ratings (or maybe you don't, but should!), this is the app for you.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}NC Food Inspector is a brand new app designed to give citizens easy access to public health scores at restaurants in our community. It was created by {% endblocktrans %}Code for Durham, {% blocktrans %}a non-profit organization committed to building helpful resources for our community out of public data.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}For now, it's simple: map restaurants according to their public health inspection scores. Eventually, we'd like to mash it up with Yelp and{% endblocktrans %} Bull City Bucks {% blocktrans %}to give an uber-Durham score that's worthy of our "Tastiest Town of the South" status. Give us feedback and keep coming back to see new iterations.{% endblocktrans %}
